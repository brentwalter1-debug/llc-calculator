Why Use an LLC Threshold Calculator?

Understanding the "LLC Threshold" is crucial for small business owners. As a sole proprietor, you are taxed on 100% of your business profit at a rate of 15.3% for self-employment taxes. This calculator helps you see the point where forming an LLC or S-Corp becomes a financially superior choice.

When should you transition?

Most CPAs suggest that once your net business profit reaches the $40,000 to $50,000 range, the tax savings of an S-Election start to outweigh the additional administrative and payroll costs. Use this tool to visualize your specific tax burden across all 50 states.
